When to Seek Help

Signs you may need professional support

  • Persistent sadness over 2 weeks
  • Withdrawing from loved ones
  • Thoughts of self-harm
  • Unable to do daily activities
🌱

Grief & Loss

Understanding your grief journey

  • The 5 stages of grief (Kübler-Ross)
  • Grief is not linear — be patient
  • Allow yourself to feel it fully
  • Connect with others who understand

Relationship Health

Building and healing connections

  • Set healthy boundaries
  • Communicate with "I feel..." statements
  • Recognize toxic patterns early
  • Self-love comes first
📚

Academic Stress

Managing pressure and burnout

  • Break tasks into small steps
  • Pomodoro technique (25min focus)
  • Sleep is non-negotiable
  • Talk to someone you trust

5 Founding Laws

1. Sacred SafetyNo one is attacked or shamed. This space is zero-tolerance.
2. Listening FirstAcknowledge the pain before giving advice. Presence matters more than solutions.
3. Honest AnonymityIdentity is your choice, never forced. Everyone starts masked.
4. ProgressStories are not meant to stay stuck. Gentle forward movement is encouraged.
5. PurposeThose who heal are quietly invited to support others still hurting.

The Majih Policy

1. About The MajihThe Majih is committed to providing compassionate listening, meaningful support and a pathway to healing for individuals in need.
2. MissionTo hear without judgement, help with compassion and heal with purpose.
3. Core ValuesCompassion, respect, confidentiality, integrity, safety and accountability.
4. Code of ConductTreat all individuals with respect and non-judgment at all times.
5. Protection of Vulnerable PersonsWe prioritise the protection of vulnerable adults, youth, minors and individuals in crisis.
6. Confidentiality & Privacy PolicyAll personal information shared with The Majih is treated as strictly confidential.
7. Ethical Listening & Support PolicyWe must: listen without interrupting or judging, empower individuals rather than create dependency, and encourage professional referrals when necessary.
8. Inclusivity PolicyThe Majih serves individuals regardless of race, gender, religion, ethnicity, disability, sexual orientation, socio-economic status or immigration status. All are welcome.
9. Data Protection PolicyAll digital records must be password protected. Personal data will not be sold or shared. Data retention periods must follow applicable laws.
