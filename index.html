THE MAJIH CONSTITUTION v1.0

5 Founding Laws

1
Law of Sacred Safety
No one is ever attacked, shamed, mocked, blamed, or humiliated here.
2
Law of Listening First
Before giving advice — acknowledge the pain. Presence matters more than solutions.
3
Law of Honest Anonymity
Everyone starts masked. Identity is your choice, never forced.
4
Law of Progress
Stories are not meant to stay stuck. Gentle forward movement is encouraged.
5
Law of Purpose
Those who heal are invited to quietly support others.
